Tips#

  • https://udhcp.busybox.net/README.udhcpc

  • https://wiki.alpinelinux.org/wiki/Udhcpc
  • signals
    • SIGUSR1 - 刷新状态，会从新获取 IP
    • SIGUSR2 - 释放当前租约，进入不活跃状态，发送 SIGUSR1 信号可激活
